It was under his guidance and strong vision that such a clinic and division was born. The division takes strength and energy from the ever growing reputation of our doctor. Considered to be among the top Gastro surgeons in the city, we offer comprehensive care for your gastro surgical ailments.

Dr. Anush Mohan

MBBS, MS, DNB, MRCS, MNAMS, FIAMS, FIAGES

With a range of medical interests including but not limited to Upper Gastrointestinal surgery, Hepatobiliary Pancreatic surgery, Colorectal surgery, Bariatric surgery, Proctology, Adrenal surgery and Advanced Laparoscopic surgery, our doctor has established himself as a leading consultant for many reputed hospitals in Trivandrum.

Whatever your surgical Gastro requirements, our specialist will guide you through the process and help you overcome the complications. It is a matter of pride and happiness to be able to help you.
